Blue

Blue. Blue is a primary color across all models of color space. It is the color of the ocean and the sky; it often symbolizes serenity, stability, inspiration, or wisdom.

Green

Green is the color most commonly associated in the United States and Europe with springtime, freshness, and hope. Green is often used to symbolize rebirth and renewal and immortality.

Red

Red is the color of strength and courage.It represents the flowing of life through one's body, giving it power. As blood flows through the heart, red is also associated with different feelings such as love, passion, and anger.
